Neuroplasticity.

what is it?

A new way of thinking about the brain essentially. Neuroplasticity takes a non deterministic view of human potential. Where the previous concensus was built on the idea of intelligence being fixed once the brain had fully developed, the newer concensus is that the brain can form new synaptic pathways and strengthen them based on intillectual and environmental inputs. Since the 70's and 80's Neuroplasticity has gained wide acceptance through the scientific community as a complicated but fundimental reality of the human brain.

"Neuroplasticity research showed that the brain changes its very structure with each different activity it performs, perfecting its circuits so it is better suited to the task at hand."

-Naveen Jain

Channelling these ideas into your life can be daunting if you view it as a whole, but I would advise anyone interested, to bite themself off small achievable chunks. You can become more neuroplastic by working out regularly, eating well, sleeping at least 8 hours a night, reading a book, travelling to another country, learning a new language etc.

"change is mostly limited to those situations which the brain is in the mood for it"

-10 fundamentals of rewiring your brain.
Debbie Hampton

What is a Growth Mindset and how can we use it?

The easiest way to think about the growth mindset is to consider what it isnt. states:

"in a fixed mindset, students believe their basic ablities, their intelligence, their talents, are just fixed traits. They have a certain amount and that's that, and then their goal becomes to look smart all the time and never to look dumb."

-Psychologist Carol Dweck

A growth mindset challenges this notion by placing emphasis and reward on effort and persistance, not the educational outcomes themselves. 'I dont know now but I will know soon' encapsulates the relevance for coding; a bottomless and everchanging branch of the tech world, where its much more important to lean the tools of learning to help you stay relevant, than it is to be infinately knowledgable using the current tools.
